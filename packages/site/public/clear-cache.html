Clear Browser Cache

If you're experiencing redirect issues or seeing old content, use these options to clear your browser cache:

Manual Steps:

  1. Chrome/Edge: Press Ctrl+Shift+Delete (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+Delete (Mac)
  2. Select "Cached images and files"
  3. Choose "All time" for time range
  4. Click "Clear data"
  5. Restart your browser
